Work Experience


•  Founder, CEO & CTO, Minus Zero

Minus Zero is a deep-tech startup working to leverage the power of moonshot innovation, off-paper research and unbiased prototyping - to an inclusive society at large. Building up on an indigenous vision of remodeling and reinforcing the harnesses of Artificial Intelligence, Robotics and Internet in India, they are engineering robust and affordable future with Self Driving Cars, Cognitive Humanoids, Generative AI, HoloVision, Artificial Brain Maps & 1000x Lossless Data Compression Codec.


•  Chief Advisor & Founding Board Member, Fermi Center for Applied Sciences

Fermi Center for Applied Sciences is an independent research entity, established is association with Minus Zero, to nurture frugal and indigenous innovation & research in India across the domains of Applied Engineering, Life Sciences and Engineering.


Current Projects


•  Fully Self Driving Car for Indian Traffic (Level 5 Autonomy)

End to end in-development fully Autonomous Electric Vehicle able to drive without human supervision in random stochastic traffic like India's. The infrastructure is computationally least expensive with less dependency on data. Affordable prototype relying broadly on computer vision. 

•  Cognitive Humanoids with Generative AI

Under-development humanoid with cognitive emotion understanding capabilities, relying on in-house Generative AI with least dependency on previous data and can think/generate rational human-centered ideas.  

•  1000x Data Compression Codec

An encoding to compress 1000 times a 4K resolution video file with negligible loss in video quality. Suitable for storage and transfer over internet.

•  HoloVision

Ability to project holographic figures into vacant space perceivable by naked eye though a certain lens. The 3D model can be interacted with using haptic feedback gloves and other human centered sensory machinery.
